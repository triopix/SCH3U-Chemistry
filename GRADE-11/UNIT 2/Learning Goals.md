>[!IMPORTANT]
>Unit 2: Nomenclature & Types of Reactions
>##### Your Learning Goals for this Unit: 
>- Understand and apply IUPAC Nomenclature Rules for Ionic and Molecular Compounds. Ionic Compounds include Simple Ionic, Multivalent Species, and Polyatomics & Derivatives.
>- Understand and apply IUPAC Nomenclature Rules for Acids & Bases 
>- Apply the Law of Conservation of Mass and Be able to Balance Chemical Reactions 
>- Classify Chemical Reactions as Synthesis, Decomposition, Single Displacement, Double Displacement, Combustion and Neutralization. Use the Reactivity Series and Solubility Table to predict products of reactions.
